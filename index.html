Profile

Shreesh Thekkatte

Theatre Artist | Director | Lighting Designer | Theatre Technician

Shreesh Thekkatte is a theatre artist and technician with experience across acting, direction, lighting design, and technical theatre. His work spans stage productions, children's theatre, repertory performances, and dance productions, joining artistic presence with backstage craft.

2016

Theatre Journey

Shreesh began his theatre career with Maduve Hennu, staged at the College Theatre Festival organized by Mysuru Rangayana.

  • Maduve Hennu - directed by Rohith S. Baikady
  • Suthina Aata - directed by Rohith S. Baikady and presented at the same festival
  • Manishada - Bhoomika Haradi production, directed by Vignesh Thekkaru
  • Maranayaka - Yashaswi Kalavrinda, Thekkatte, directed by Rohith S. Baikady
  • Urubhanga - directed by Prashanth Udyavara
2018-2019

Professional Training

Completed a Diploma in Dramatic Arts from Ranga Adyana Kendra, Bhandarkars' College, Kundapura.

  • Antigone (Greek tragedy) - directed by Keerthi Prasad
  • Konege Sikkiddenu (based on stories by Saadat Hasan Manto) - directed by Bhuvan Manipal
  • Praja Purana - directed by Kumar Lal Saab Nadaf
  • Madhyamavyaoga by Bhasa - directed by Klippa Poojary
Acting

Acting Experience

Following his diploma, Shreesh worked with Koshika Chekadi troupe and later served as a repertory artist with Shivamogga Rangayana for two years.

  • Sattare Andre Saaytare - directed by Rohith S. Baikady
  • Kudure Bandu Kudure - directed by Rohith S. Baikady
  • Chanakya Prapancha - directed by Venkataramana Aithal
  • Hakki Kathe - directed by Shravan Heggodu and Ganesh Mandarthi
  • Vidhurashwatha - directed by Krishnamurthy Kavathar
  • Post Box No. 9 - directed by Raghu Purappemane
  • Chitrapata Ramayana (Yakshagana production) - directed by Shailesh Thirthahalli
Direction

Direction

Directed theatre productions for children's theatre initiatives and educational theatre programs.

  • Lambakarnana Ushneesha - Children's Summer Camp, Shivamogga Rangayana
  • Gubbi Kathe - Children's Summer Camp, Shivamogga Rangayana
  • Lambakarnana Ushneesha - Rangabhumi Ranga Shikshana Abhiyana, School Theatre Program
Lighting

Lighting Design & Technical Theatre

Alongside his performance work, Shreesh has built extensive experience as a lighting designer and theatre technician.

  • Sevanthi Prasanga - Yashaswi Kalavrinda, Thekkatte
  • Alilu Ramayana - directed by Prashanth Kota
  • Kunikuni Navile - directed by Rohith S. Baikady
  • Lambakarnana Ushneesha - directed by Ranjith S. Kakkade
  • Heddayana - directed by Ashok Mysuru (Neenasam)
  • Chennabhairadevi - directed by Nagesh
  • Maduve Hennu - Rithumana Ranga Shibira, Kalabhi (R), Mangaluru
  • Moggli - Kalabhi Children's Theatre
Dance

Dance Productions

  • Lighting design and operation for numerous productions by Araholle Pratishtana and Nritya Niketana Kadavoor (R)
  • Lighting designer for Shiva Tandava by Nritya Niketana Udupi (R)
